How it Works

The Arsenal

BattleTags is an ongoing match-play war. Every player starts the season armed with exactly 26 physical Tags.

The Challenge

Challenge anyone to head-to-head Match Play. Win holes, go up. Lose holes, go down.

The Breakfast Ball

Every player gets a breakfast ball on the first hole. However, choose wisely: if you elect to hit a second ball, you are committed to that second ball. No playing your first!

To The Victor Go The Spoils

Win the match, capture a Tag. The winner logs the victory to update the Damage Report.

Pics or it Didn't Happen

When submitting a win, uploading photographic evidence of the post-match handshake (or the loser handing over the tag) is highly encouraged. It gets added to the Photoboard Gallery for everyone to see.

The Glory

The live standings track every match played, tags won, and tags lost. The player with the most Tags won by the end of the season takes ultimate bragging rights.

The Payout

At the end of the season, the top three players with the most Tags won will take home the prize pool. Payouts are distributed as 60% for 1st place, 30% for 2nd place, and 10% for 3rd place.
